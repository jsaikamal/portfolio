About Me

Hi, I’m Jsai Kamal — a passionate Graphic Designer, Photo & Video Editor, and aspiring Front-End Developer.

I love transforming ideas into visually compelling designs and interactive digital experiences. My work blends creativity with technical skills, whether it’s making posters, thumbnails, cinematic edits, or building sleek web portfolios.

🚀 Skills & Tools: Adobe Photoshop, Premiere Pro, After Effects, Canva, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and UI/UX design basics.

🎯 My goal is to create designs and websites that are not only attractive but also user-friendly, responsive, and impactful.

“Design is not just what it looks like — it’s how it makes people feel.”
